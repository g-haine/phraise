## Abstract
In this article, we present a unified framework of discontinuous Galerkin (DG) discretizations for Hamiltonian ODEs and PDEs. We show that with appropriate numerical fluxes the numerical algorithms deduced from DG discretizations can be combined with the symplectic methods in time to derive the multi-symplectic PRK schemes. The resulting numerical discretizations are applied to the linear and nonlinear Schr√∂dinger equations. Some conservative properties of the numerical schemes are investigated and confirmed in the numerical experiments.
